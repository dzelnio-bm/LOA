About Lincoln Ostomy Association

We'll walk with you through your ostomy journey.

Lincoln Ostomy Association provides tools for ostomates. According to Mayo Clinic, it takes time to be comfortable with an ostomy — connecting with community can help. The United Ostomy Association estimates 500–800,000 people have a stoma in the U.S. and 120,000 new surgeries are performed each year.

  • Do many of the same activities you enjoyed before your surgery.
  • Ask questions you might be embarrassed to ask your doctor or nurse.
  • Get tips to help you adapt to life with an ostomy.
